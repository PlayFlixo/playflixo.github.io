TI-84 Plus CE Calculator Games

Please Note: Computer that runs Windows or Macintosh is required.

Instructions:

  1. First, you are going to need to download the TI Connect CE software. You can download them below.
  2. Connect your calculator to your computer using the included cable or a USB-A to Mini-B cable. Make sure the calculator is on.
  3. If you just recently purchased your TI-84 Plus CE Graphing Calculator, you're probably going to need to jailbreak it first. If your calculator is a hand-me-down, you can probably skip the next step.
  4. Open the TI Connect CE software. Leave it opened in the background. Download both of the Jailbreaking files from below. Now open your files app, and select both files you just downloaded. Drag and drop them into the TI Connect CE software you left open in the background. You have just jailbroken your calculator.
  5. Next, you are going to download the games you want to play. Download the games from below.
  6. Now you have to decompress the files. Open your files app, and open the zip file you just downloaded. Now click and drag your cursor across the files inside of the zip. Drag all of the files out of the file app and drag them onto your desktop/home screen. You have just decompressed the ZIP file.
  7. Open the TI Connect CE software in the back so you are still able to see the files you just added to your home screen. Now you are going to want to select all of the files you just added to your desktop/home screen and drag them into your TI Connect CE app. Click run or whatever the button says when the popup shows.
  8. You have downloaded your game(s). To play your game(s), click apps on your calculator, then CalcPlex. Bam, games for your boring math class.
